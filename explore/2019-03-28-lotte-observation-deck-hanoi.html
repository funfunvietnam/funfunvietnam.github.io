Lotte Observation Deck, Hanoi

Hanoi
tour

One of the most popular spot for foreign travelers to visit a big city is an elevated sightseeing platform. It provides a great way to observe an overview of the city and also help people to check a bucket list for their trips. 🤗

# Lotte Center Hanoi

Lotte Center Hanoi is a skyscraper in Ba Đình, Hanoi, Vietnam. It completed on 2 September 2014 and has 65 floors. This tower is the second tallest building in Hanoi and the third tallest building in Vietnam. Lotte Center Hanoi includes offices, a 5 star hotel, a 6th floor Lotte Department Store, serviced residences, the Evian Spa, the Lotte Mart, restaurants and an observation deck.

# Observation Deck

# The Entrance

The entrance is at B1 floor. You can buy ticket here and take a high speed elevator to the observation deck, on 65th floor, within 1 minute.
