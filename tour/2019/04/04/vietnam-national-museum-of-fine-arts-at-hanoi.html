Vietnam National Museum of Fine Arts at Hanoi

April 4, 2019

The Vietnam National Museum of Fine Arts (Bảo tàng Mỹ thuật Việt Nam) is the biggest museum in Vietnam. The second one is Ho Chi Minh City Museum of Fine Arts. Since 1966, the Vietnam National Fine Arts Museum officially opened to the public. The entire area of the museum is 4,737m2 of which the exhibition space is around 3,000m2.

The mission of the Vietnam National Museum of Fine Arts is to preserve, display and inform, building on a keen commitment to research and conservation, and to the sharing of all there is to learn and appreciate about the enduring artistic heritage of the Vietnamese people.

The Visit

Opening Hours

  • 08:30 - 17:00 seven days a week, except for Tet Holidays (the Lunar New Year).

Ticket Prices

  • $40,000 VND for adults
  • $20,000 VND for College and University students (with cards issued by Vietnamese higher education institutions only)
  • $10,000 VND for children between the ages of 6 and 16
  • Free for children under 6 years old

Tour Guides

  • Fee: $150,000 VND/group (25 persons/group maximum)
  • Languages: Vietnamese, English, French, Chinese
  • Duration: Upon request (except lunch time, from 11:30 to 13:30)
  • Tours
    • General tour of the Museum
    • Thematic tours:
      • Learning tradition
      • Vietnamese countryside – A perspective
      • Vietnamese find arts – Treasure in the heart of Hanoi
    • Collections tours (reservation in advance):
      • Ancient art
      • Modern art
      • Applied art
      • Folk art
      • Ceramic collection
    • Contact: Tour guide Team, Department of Exhibition and Education
      • (84-24) 3823-3084, 3733-2131 ext. 131

Educational Programmes at the Creative Space for Children

  • Regular programme: $50.000VND/ticket
    • Timetable: Morning: 9:00 to 11:30 - Afternoon: 14:00 to 16:30
    • Children from 5 - 15 years old
  • Special programme (advanced booking required): $50.000VND/ticket
    • Timetable: Morning: 9:00 to 11:30 - Afternoon: 14:00 to 16:30
    • Children from 5 - 15 years old

Location

The address of Vietnam National Museum of Fine Arts is 66 Nguyen Thai Hoc, Ba Dinh, Hanoi, Vietnam.

You can try to use Grab Service 🚕 to arrive here. Convenient and great experience.
