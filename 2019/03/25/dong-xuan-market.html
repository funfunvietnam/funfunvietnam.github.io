Dong Xuan Market

March 25, 2019

Dong Xuan Market (Chợ Đồng Xuân) is the largest wholesale market in Northern Vietnam. Built in 1889, through renovated several times with the latest in 1994. Trading goods include everything from clothes, household goods, electronics to foodstuffs. If you are a tourist, you can find some interested souvenirs in here. It is located in the old quarter of Hanoi capital. 1km distance to Hoan Kiem Lake.

Goods in each floors

  • First floor
    • electronics
    • household goods
    • foodstuffs
    • watches
    • backpacks
    • bags
    • wallets
    • souvenirs
  • Second floor
    • clothes for adult
    • fabric, cloth
  • Third floor
    • children’s clothes
    • fashion clothes for both men and women of middle age

Business Hours

Thursday7AM - 6PM
Friday7AM - 12AM
Saturday7AM - 12AM
Sunday7AM - 12AM

Location

The address of Dong Xuan Market is Đồng Xuân, Hoàn Kiếm, Hanoi, Vietnam. Located at the old quarter of Hanoi capital.
